Our Story

Blossom Bae began in a classroom of RIT with five dying ferns(yeahh us) and a determination to figure out what went wrong(commonsense?). What started as a personal quest(Project that we had to do) to keep a few houseplants(does vaazha count?) alive blossomed into a passion for urban botany and biophilic design.

Today, we are a the same bunch of Musaceae , dedicated to reconnecting city dwellers with nature. We believe that incorporating living elements into our personal spaces isn't just about aesthetics it's a critical component of modern well-being.

Our Mission

To make the transformative power of plants accessible, sustainable, and beautiful for every living space, regardless of the size or the owner's experience level.
